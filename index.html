VLEs typically include features such as course management, content delivery, communication tools (such as discussion forums, chat rooms, and messaging), assessment tools, and sometimes multimedia content. They are commonly used in distance learning, online courses, and blended learning environments where both traditional classroom instruction and online learning are combined. Examples of VLEs include Moodle, Blackboard, Canvas, and Google Classroom.A Virtual Learning Environment (VLE) is an online platform or system that facilitates education by allowing teachers and students to interact, share resources, collaborate on projects, and engage in various learning activities in a virtual space.
